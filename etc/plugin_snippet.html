Adding Zilverline Search Plugin to Your Browser

Zilverline Search Plugin works with the Mozilla family of browsers. By adding the Zilverline Search Plugin, you will be able to search your bookmarks from your browser, as shown in this screenshot.

Installing

Install the plugin with a single click. No computer or browser restart necessary.

Hint: once you have installed the plugin, you can use Ctrl-K (UNIX and Windows) or Command-K (OS X) to quickly jump to the plugin search field.

Uninstalling

  1. Go to the installation directory of your browser.
  2. Locate the sub-directory called searchplugins.
  3. Delete both zilverline.src and zilverline.gif files.
  4. Restart your browser and the Zilverline Search Plugin will disappear.
